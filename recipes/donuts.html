Donuts

Description

These are Odin Recipes' World Famous donuts! A very sweet and wonderful pastry for all to enjoy, you are sure to be overjoyed and excited to eat these donuts!

Topped with sugary glazes and a beautiful selection of chocolates, sprinkles, and cookie crumbs! Try it out today using the recipe below!

Ingredients

  • Sugar/Powdered Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • Nutmeg
  • Baking Powder
  • Flavorings of your choice! (could be a filling or something to drizzle over the top)

Steps

  1. Mix the sugar and butter until thick
  2. Combine flour, salt, nutmeg, and baking powder to prepare the dough and refrigerate covered for 2 hours
  3. Once out of the fridge, cut donut shapes on floured surface (prevents dough from being stuck to counter)
  4. Frying time! Fry donuts for 3-5 minutes at 375 degrees fahrenheit until both sides are golden brown
  5. Leave donuts on a baking sheet lined with a cooling rack and once fully cooled, decorate and enhance the flavors however you desire!

Conclusion

And there you go! By the end of this recipe, you should have been presented with a masterfully crafted tray of incredibly delicious donut pastries! They might not look the exact same as the pictures, but the sediment is the same, you made them! Feel proud and enjoy the donuts you made with friends and family!

Cookies

Description

These are Odin Recipes' amazing chocolate chip cookies! Soft, chewy and a splendid snack for all to enjoy, you won't regret making these cookies!

Generous with rich and creamy chocolate chip pieces and freshly baked with love and care everytime! Try it out today using the recipe below!

Ingredients

  • 2 Mixing Bowls (large and small)
  • Wisk
  • Mixer
  • Granulated Sugar/Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Cooking Spray
  • Baking Powder
  • And of course, the chocolate chips!

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Combine dry ingredients into the small bowl and wet ingredients into the large bowl
  3. Add eggs one at a time into wet mixture and beat mixture with each egg addition. Once done, combine wet and dry mixtures
  4. Time to add the chips! Pour desired amount of chocolate chips into mixture and stir into dough
  5. Baking time! Scoop cookies onto a sprayed pan and bake in the oven for around 8-10 minutes
  6. Once cooked and toasted, transfer cookies onto cooling rack and repeat steps 2-4 with leftover dough (if desired)

Conclusion

And there you have it, Odin Recipes' wonderful chocolate chip cookies! By the end of this recipe, you should have ended up with a fresh, tasty tray of homemade cookies!

Muffins

Description

These are Odin Recipes' warm and comforting muffins! Wonderful and rich in taste, slightly crumbly but moist and flavorful, you will definitely fall in love with these muffins!

A soft, bready exterior, topped with beautiful berries that'll make your day! Try it out today using the recipe below!

Ingredients

  • Muffin Pan
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • Muffin Liners
  • Baking Powder
  • Mix-ins of your choice! (could be a filling or something to add on over the top when muffins are baking)

Steps

  1. Preheat pan and oven at 375 degrees fahrenheit and line pan with muffin liners
  2. Mix dry ingredients and wet ingredients seperately and afterwards, slowly fold wet mixture into dry mixture
  3. Time to add the mix-ins! Slowly add your desired mix-in into the combined mixture 4-5 times to fully coat
  4. Time to bake! Fill about 1/3 of each slot in the pan with the muffin mixture and bake until golden brown or around 18-20 minutes

Conclusion

And there you have it! By the end of this recipe, you should have made some real stand up muffins that taste breathtaking with fragrance! They might not be as the pictures make them out to look, but everyone starts somewhere! Feel proud and enjoy the muffins you made yourself or with friends and family!
